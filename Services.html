Services

1

Integrated Penetration Testing

This is our area of expertise, we can check your site(s) for all the latest vulnerabilities and exploits. This includes SQL Injection testing, Cross-Site Scripting (XSS), Broken Authentication & Session Management, LDAP Injection, Insecure Direct Object Reference, Security Misconfigurations, Cross Site Request Forgery (CSRF or XSRF), we also check for possible Unvalidated Redirects & Forwards. And just about every other way a hacker could gain access to your site and valuable information.

2

Stress Testing

We also offer 160Gbps Stress Testing. This is to see if your site is DDoS protected, good DDoS protection will be able to recognize the attack and mitigate the traffic while simultaneously banning the IP addresses.

3

Easy to Moderate Fixes

Any vulnerabilities we may encounter will be sent in a list back to you, also with whether or not we can fix them for a small fee. If we are unable to fix them we will recommend other companies to you and not charge anything for the fixing fee.
